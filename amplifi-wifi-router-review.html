amplifi-hd wifi router

by Ubiquiti Labs

A wifi router with unprecedented speeds

Wifi routers have always been a bulky (and sometimes an alien shaped) piece of hardware that people are tired to hide. That’s why Ubiquiti Labs designed this modern beauty piece of tech art that not only combines a clean design, but also a combination of a powerful mesh technology system with a seamless bluetooth integration in a friendly software app.

What I liked

Clean, modern & robust design Serious horsepower & solid performance at both 2Ghz and 5Ghz ranges Beautiful multicolored LCD display showing the speeds stats & a thin white LED strim that pulses when there’s network activity Supports up to 10 extra antennas in a single mesh network, and can cover up to 20,000 sq ft with just the two included ones 4 LAN ports, 1 WAN port, and a USB 2.0 port Easy install, setup and managment through the phone (iOS and Android) or Desktop app.

What I didn't

Lack of Quality of Service (QoS) and an onboard firewall The HD model can be a bit pricey for some users Only two colors so far: black and white

Ubiquiti AmpliFi HD: Specs

  • Mesh networking kit
  • 1x router, 2x mesh points
  • Interfaces: Router – 4x Gigabit Ethernet, 1x WAN, 1x USB-C (power)
  • Wi-fi: Dual-band 802.11ac (2.4GHz/5.0GHz), 3x3MIMO
  • Dimensions: Router – 100x100x100mm
  • Mesh points - 245x55x30mm
